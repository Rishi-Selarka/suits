COMMERCIAL LEASE AGREEMENT

This Commercial Lease Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on 1st April 2026 between:

LANDLORD: Mr. Harish Chandra Joshi, residing at 4, Civil Lines, Allahabad – 211001 ("Landlord")
TENANT: Bloom Retail Solutions Pvt. Ltd., having its registered office at 88, Industrial Area Phase 2, Chandigarh – 160002 ("Tenant")

1. PREMISES
The Landlord agrees to lease the commercial premises situated at Ground Floor, Plot No. 14, Sector 17-C, Chandigarh – 160017, admeasuring 1,800 sq. ft. ("Premises") to the Tenant for commercial use only.

2. TERM
2.1 The lease shall be for an initial term of 3 years commencing from 1st April 2026 ("Lock-in Period").
2.2 During the Lock-in Period, the Tenant shall not vacate the Premises without paying rent for the remainder of the Lock-in Period as liquidated damages.
2.3 After the Lock-in Period, the lease may be renewed by mutual written agreement.

3. RENT
3.1 Monthly Rent: INR 75,000 per month.
3.2 Rent is payable by the 5th of each month in advance.
3.3 Rent Escalation: 10% increase at the end of every 12 months.
3.4 Late payment: Penalty of INR 1,500 per day after the 7th of the month.
3.5 Rent shall be paid regardless of whether the Tenant is conducting business from the Premises.

4. SECURITY DEPOSIT
4.1 The Tenant has paid a security deposit of INR 2,25,000 (3 months' rent) to the Landlord, receipt of which is acknowledged.
4.2 The security deposit shall be refunded within 60 days of vacating, subject to deductions for damages, unpaid dues, or breach.
4.3 The security deposit shall not carry any interest.
4.4 The Landlord may invoke the security deposit without notice to cover unpaid rent or repair costs.

5. PERMITTED USE
The Premises shall be used exclusively for retail trade. Any change in use requires prior written consent of the Landlord.

6. MAINTENANCE AND REPAIRS
6.1 The Tenant shall maintain the Premises in good condition and promptly repair minor damages.
6.2 Structural repairs and major maintenance are the Landlord's responsibility.
6.3 The Tenant shall not make any structural alterations without prior written consent. Approved alterations shall become part of the Premises and property of the Landlord upon vacation.
6.4 The Tenant is responsible for all utility connections, payments, and related repairs.

7. SUBLETTING
The Tenant shall not sublet, assign, or part with possession of the Premises or any part thereof without prior written consent of the Landlord. Unauthorized subletting shall entitle the Landlord to terminate immediately.

8. INSURANCE
The Tenant shall maintain adequate insurance for its goods, assets, and third-party liability. The Landlord shall not be responsible for any loss of Tenant's property.

9. TERMINATION
9.1 After the Lock-in Period, either party may terminate with 3 months' written notice.
9.2 The Landlord may terminate immediately for: non-payment of rent for 15+ days, unauthorized subletting, change of use, or insolvency.
9.3 Upon termination, the Tenant shall vacate within 30 days and hand over the Premises in original condition, fair wear and tear excepted.

10. INSPECTION
The Landlord or his representative may inspect the Premises at any time with 24 hours' prior notice.

11. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement is governed by the Transfer of Property Act, 1882 and laws of India applicable in Chandigarh. Disputes shall be subject to courts in Chandigarh.
