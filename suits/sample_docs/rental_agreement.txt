RESIDENTIAL LEAVE AND LICENSE AGREEMENT

This Leave and License Agreement ("Agreement") is executed on the 1st day of January, 2025, at Bangalore, Karnataka.

BETWEEN:
Mr. Rajesh Kumar (hereinafter referred to as "Licensor/Owner"), having address at No. 42, 4th Cross, Indiranagar, Bangalore - 560038

AND

Ms. Priya Sharma (hereinafter referred to as "Licensee/Tenant"), having address at No. 15, MG Road, Bangalore - 560001

WHEREAS the Licensor is the absolute owner of the residential premises situated at Flat No. 301, 3rd Floor, Sunshine Apartments, HSR Layout, Bangalore - 560102 ("the Premises") and the Licensee desires to take the said premises on leave and license basis.

1. TERM AND DURATION
This Agreement shall be valid for a period of 11 (eleven) months commencing from 1st January 2025 to 30th November 2025. The agreement shall not be renewed automatically. Either party must provide 2 months written notice for renewal.

2. LICENSE FEE (RENT)
The Licensee shall pay a monthly license fee of Rs. 25,000/- (Rupees Twenty-Five Thousand only) payable on or before the 5th day of every calendar month. A late payment penalty of Rs. 100 per day shall apply for payments received after the 10th of each month.

3. SECURITY DEPOSIT
The Licensee has paid a refundable security deposit of Rs. 1,50,000/- (Rupees One Lakh Fifty Thousand only) equivalent to 6 months rent. The deposit shall be refunded within 90 days of vacating the premises, after deducting any outstanding dues or damages.

4. ANNUAL RENT ESCALATION
The Licensor reserves the right to increase the license fee by up to 15% annually upon providing 15 days written notice. The Licensee must accept the revised rent or vacate the premises within 30 days of receiving such notice.

5. MAINTENANCE AND REPAIRS
The Licensee shall bear all costs of minor repairs up to Rs. 10,000 per instance. For repairs exceeding Rs. 10,000, the Licensor shall be responsible. The Licensee shall maintain the premises in good habitable condition and shall not make any structural alterations without written consent.

6. ENTRY AND INSPECTION
The Licensor or his authorized representative shall have the right to enter and inspect the premises at any time without prior notice for the purpose of inspection, repairs, or any other purpose deemed necessary by the Licensor.

7. INDEMNIFICATION
The Licensee shall indemnify and hold harmless the Licensor against ALL claims, damages, losses, costs, and expenses of whatever nature arising from the Licensee's use of the premises, INCLUDING those arising from the Licensor's own negligence or failure to maintain the structural integrity of the building.

8. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this Agreement by giving 2 months written notice. However, the Licensor may terminate immediately without notice if the Licensee violates any term of this Agreement. The Licensee shall have no right to early termination during the first 6 months (lock-in period). Early termination by the Licensee during the lock-in period shall result in forfeiture of the entire security deposit.

9. USE OF PREMISES
The premises shall be used solely for residential purposes. The Licensee shall not sublet, assign, or transfer this license to any third party. No pets are allowed on the premises. The Licensee shall not use the premises for any illegal or immoral purpose.

10. IMPROVEMENTS AND FIXTURES
Upon termination, any improvements, alterations, or fixtures installed by the Licensee shall become the absolute property of the Licensor without any compensation to the Licensee, regardless of the value of such improvements.

11. NON-COMPETE AND RESTRICTION
The Licensee shall not operate any business, whether commercial or otherwise, from the premises or within a 5 kilometer radius of the premises for a period of 2 years after vacating, that competes with any business interests of the Licensor.

12. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
All disputes arising out of this Agreement shall be subject to arbitration by a sole arbitrator appointed by the Licensor. The arbitration shall be conducted in accordance with the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996. The venue of arbitration shall be Bangalore. The costs of arbitration shall be borne entirely by the Licensee regardless of the outcome.

13. GOVERNING LAW AND JURISDICTION
This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of India. The courts at Bangalore shall have exclusive jurisdiction over any disputes arising out of this Agreement.

14. WAIVER OF RIGHTS
The Licensee hereby waives the right to claim any statutory protections under the Karnataka Rent Control Act or any other tenant protection legislation, to the extent permissible by law.

15. ENTIRE AGREEMENT AND AMENDMENT
This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties. The Licensor reserves the right to amend any terms of this Agreement at any time by providing written notice to the Licensee. The Licensee's continued occupation of the premises after receiving such notice shall constitute acceptance of the amended terms.
