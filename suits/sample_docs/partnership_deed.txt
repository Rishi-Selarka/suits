PARTNERSHIP DEED

This Partnership Deed ("Deed") is executed on 1st February 2026 between:

1. Mr. Vikram Sharma, S/o Mr. Rajesh Sharma, residing at 22, Napier Road, Lucknow – 226001 ("Partner 1")
2. Ms. Ananya Singh, D/o Mr. Harish Singh, residing at 14, Gomti Nagar Extension, Lucknow – 226010 ("Partner 2")
3. Mr. Sanjay Tiwari, S/o Mr. Mohan Tiwari, residing at 7, Hazratganj, Lucknow – 226001 ("Partner 3")

(Collectively referred to as "Partners")

RECITALS
The Partners wish to form a partnership firm for the purpose of carrying on the business of restaurant and catering services under the name "The Lucknow Kitchen" ("Firm").

1. FIRM NAME AND BUSINESS
1.1 The Firm shall operate under the name "The Lucknow Kitchen."
1.2 The principal place of business shall be at 56, Hazratganj, Lucknow – 226001.
1.3 The Firm may open branches at other locations as mutually agreed.

2. COMMENCEMENT
The partnership shall be deemed to have commenced on 1st February 2026.

3. CAPITAL CONTRIBUTION
3.1 The Partners shall contribute the following capital:
    - Partner 1: INR 20,00,000
    - Partner 2: INR 15,00,000
    - Partner 3: INR 10,00,000
    Total Capital: INR 45,00,000
3.2 Additional capital may be introduced by any Partner with consent of all other Partners.
3.3 No Partner shall withdraw capital without unanimous consent.

4. PROFIT AND LOSS SHARING
4.1 Profits and losses shall be shared in the following ratio:
    - Partner 1: 44.4%
    - Partner 2: 33.3%
    - Partner 3: 22.2%
4.2 The profit-sharing ratio may be revised with written consent of all Partners.
4.3 Profits shall be distributed quarterly after audit.

5. PARTNER SALARIES AND DRAWINGS
5.1 Each Partner shall be entitled to a monthly drawing of INR 50,000, irrespective of profitability.
5.2 Excess drawings shall be treated as interest-free loans from the Firm and adjusted against profit distribution.

6. MANAGEMENT AND VOTING
6.1 Day-to-day management shall be handled by Partner 1 as Managing Partner.
6.2 Decisions on expenditures below INR 1,00,000 may be taken by the Managing Partner unilaterally.
6.3 Decisions on expenditures above INR 1,00,000, new contracts, and policy changes require majority vote (at least 2 of 3 Partners).
6.4 Admission of a new partner, dissolution, or sale of the Firm requires unanimous consent.

7. BANK ACCOUNTS
All Firm accounts shall require signatures of at least two Partners for transactions above INR 50,000.

8. BOOKS OF ACCOUNTS
The Firm shall maintain proper books of accounts at the principal place of business. Each Partner has the right to inspect accounts at any time.

9. INTEREST ON CAPITAL
Partners shall be entitled to interest on capital at 12% per annum, charged to Firm expenses before profit calculation.

10. RETIREMENT OF A PARTNER
10.1 A Partner may retire by giving 3 months' written notice to the other Partners.
10.2 The retiring Partner shall be entitled to their share of capital and accumulated profits as of the retirement date.
10.3 Settlement shall be completed within 6 months of retirement.
10.4 The retiring Partner shall not carry on a competing business within 50 km of Lucknow for 2 years post-retirement.

11. DEATH OR INSOLVENCY
11.1 The Firm shall not dissolve upon the death or insolvency of a Partner.
11.2 The legal heirs of a deceased Partner shall be entitled to the deceased's capital and profit share but shall not become Partners unless all remaining Partners consent.

12. EXPULSION OF A PARTNER
A Partner may be expelled by unanimous vote of the remaining Partners for:
(a) Fraud or gross misconduct affecting the Firm
(b) Willful breach of this Deed
(c) Conviction of a criminal offence

13. DISSOLUTION
13.1 The Firm may be dissolved by mutual written consent of all Partners.
13.2 Upon dissolution, assets shall be liquidated, liabilities settled, and remaining funds distributed in the profit-sharing ratio.

14. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
Disputes between Partners shall be referred to arbitration in Lucknow before a sole arbitrator under the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996.

15. GOVERNING LAW
This Deed is governed by the Indian Partnership Act, 1932 and other applicable laws of India.
