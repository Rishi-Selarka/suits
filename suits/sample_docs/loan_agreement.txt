LOAN AGREEMENT

This Loan Agreement ("Agreement") is executed on 5th January 2026 between:

LENDER: Pinnacle Finance Ltd., NBFC registered with RBI, having its office at 7, Nariman Point, Mumbai – 400021 ("Lender")
BORROWER: Mr. Rakesh Gupta, S/o Mr. Suresh Gupta, residing at 45, Sector 15, Noida, Uttar Pradesh – 201301 ("Borrower")

1. LOAN AMOUNT
The Lender agrees to lend INR 15,00,000 (Rupees Fifteen Lakhs) to the Borrower ("Principal") subject to the terms of this Agreement.

2. INTEREST RATE
2.1 The loan shall carry interest at 18% per annum, calculated on a reducing balance basis.
2.2 In case of default, penal interest at 36% per annum shall apply on overdue amounts from the date of default.
2.3 The Lender reserves the right to revise the interest rate with 30 days' prior notice. The Borrower's continued use of the loan after notice constitutes acceptance.

3. REPAYMENT
3.1 The loan shall be repaid in 36 equal monthly instalments (EMIs) of INR 54,225 each, commencing from 5th February 2026.
3.2 EMIs are due on the 5th of every month. A grace period of 3 days is provided.
3.3 Prepayment is allowed with a prepayment penalty of 3% of the outstanding principal.
3.4 Partial prepayments shall first be applied to outstanding interest, then principal.

4. SECURITY / COLLATERAL
4.1 As security for this loan, the Borrower pledges his residential property at 45, Sector 15, Noida (Property).
4.2 The Borrower shall not sell, transfer, or encumber the Property without written consent of the Lender.
4.3 The Borrower shall maintain adequate insurance on the Property and assign the policy to the Lender.

5. DEFAULT
5.1 The Borrower shall be in default if:
    (a) Any EMI is unpaid for more than 15 days after the due date
    (b) The Borrower files for insolvency or bankruptcy
    (c) The Borrower misrepresents any information in this Agreement
    (d) The Property is sold or encumbered without consent
5.2 Upon default, the entire outstanding loan amount becomes immediately due and payable (acceleration clause).
5.3 The Lender may initiate proceedings under the SARFAESI Act, 2002 to recover dues without court intervention.

6. PROCESSING FEE AND CHARGES
6.1 Processing Fee: 2% of loan amount (INR 30,000), deducted upfront.
6.2 Documentation charges: INR 5,000.
6.3 Late payment fee: INR 1,500 per overdue EMI, in addition to penal interest.
6.4 Cheque bounce charges: INR 500 per instance.

7. REPRESENTATIONS BY BORROWER
The Borrower represents and warrants that:
(a) He has full legal capacity to enter this Agreement
(b) No existing litigation or encumbrance affects the collateral property
(c) All information provided is true and accurate

8. CROSS-DEFAULT
Default under any other loan or credit facility of the Borrower shall constitute a default under this Agreement.

9. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement is governed by the laws of India. Disputes shall be resolved by the courts of Mumbai, Maharashtra.

10. ENTIRE AGREEMENT
This Agreement, along with the sanction letter and security documents, constitutes the entire agreement between the parties.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement on the date first written above.
