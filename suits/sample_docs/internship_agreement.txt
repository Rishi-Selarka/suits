INTERNSHIP AGREEMENT

This Internship Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on 1st June 2026 between:

COMPANY: PixelForge Studios Pvt. Ltd., 3rd Floor, DLF Cyber Hub, Gurugram, Haryana – 122002 ("Company")
INTERN: Ms. Riya Kapoor, D/o Mr. Anil Kapoor, residing at 34, Sector 45, Gurugram – 122003 ("Intern")

1. INTERNSHIP PERIOD
The internship shall commence on 15th June 2026 and conclude on 14th September 2026 (3 months), unless terminated earlier.

2. ROLE AND RESPONSIBILITIES
The Intern shall work as a Graphic Design Intern under the supervision of the Creative Director. Responsibilities include:
- Creating visual assets for social media and marketing campaigns
- Assisting senior designers on live client projects
- Attending and participating in team meetings
- Completing tasks assigned within agreed timelines

3. STIPEND
3.1 The Intern shall receive a monthly stipend of INR 12,000, paid by the last working day of each month.
3.2 The stipend is not a salary and does not entitle the Intern to any employment benefits.
3.3 No stipend shall be paid for days absent without prior approval.

4. WORKING HOURS
The Intern shall work Monday to Friday, 9:30 AM to 6:30 PM. Occasional weekend work may be required during project deadlines without additional compensation.

5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
All work created by the Intern during the internship, using Company resources or in connection with Company projects, shall be the exclusive property of the Company. The Intern waives any moral rights in such work.

6. CONFIDENTIALITY
The Intern shall not disclose any client data, project details, or Company information to any third party, during or after the internship. This obligation survives termination of this Agreement for 2 years.

7. SOCIAL MEDIA AND CONDUCT
The Intern shall not post, tweet, or share any Company-related content on social media without prior written approval. Violation shall result in immediate termination.

8. NO EMPLOYMENT GUARANTEE
Completion of the internship does not guarantee employment with the Company. Any offer of employment shall be a separate agreement.

9. CERTIFICATE
Upon satisfactory completion, the Company shall issue an internship completion certificate within 30 days.

10. TERMINATION
10.1 The Company may terminate this Agreement at any time without notice or reason.
10.2 The Intern may terminate with 7 days' written notice.
10.3 Upon termination, the Intern shall return all Company assets, devices, and materials immediately.

11. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement is governed by the laws of India. Any disputes shall be subject to the jurisdiction of courts in Gurugram.
