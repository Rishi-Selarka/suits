CO-FOUNDER AGREEMENT

This Co-Founder Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on 1st March 2026 between:

1. Mr. Aditya Raj, residing at 5, Indiranagar, Bengaluru – 560038 ("Co-Founder 1")
2. Ms. Shruti Menon, residing at 22, Koramangala 7th Block, Bengaluru – 560095 ("Co-Founder 2")

(Collectively "Co-Founders") in respect of the startup venture to be incorporated as Nexus AI Pvt. Ltd. ("Company").

1. ROLES AND RESPONSIBILITIES
1.1 Co-Founder 1 (Aditya Raj) — Chief Executive Officer (CEO)
    - Overall business strategy, fundraising, investor relations, sales
1.2 Co-Founder 2 (Shruti Menon) — Chief Technology Officer (CTO)
    - Product development, engineering team, technical architecture
1.3 Each Co-Founder shall devote 100% of their professional time to the Company.

2. EQUITY SPLIT
2.1 Initial equity allocation:
    - Co-Founder 1: 55%
    - Co-Founder 2: 45%
2.2 All equity is subject to a 4-year vesting schedule with a 1-year cliff:
    - 25% vests after 12 months of continuous service
    - Remaining 75% vests monthly over the following 36 months (2.08% per month)
2.3 Unvested equity is forfeited upon departure.

3. VESTING ACCELERATION
3.1 Single Trigger: In the event of acquisition of the Company, 50% of unvested equity accelerates for each Co-Founder.
3.2 Double Trigger: If a Co-Founder is terminated without cause within 12 months of an acquisition, 100% of unvested equity accelerates.

4. COMPENSATION
4.1 No salary shall be drawn by the Co-Founders until the Company raises seed funding of at least INR 1 Crore.
4.2 Post-funding, salaries shall be set by the Board of Directors.

5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY ASSIGNMENT
5.1 Each Co-Founder irrevocably assigns all IP created in connection with the Company's business to the Company, including prior work related to the venture as listed in Annexure A.
5.2 This assignment applies to all inventions, code, designs, and trade secrets created during the term of this Agreement.

6. DECISION MAKING
6.1 Day-to-day operational decisions may be taken by respective Co-Founders within their domain.
6.2 Strategic decisions (new hiring above INR 15 LPA, large expenditures, new business lines) require consent of both Co-Founders.
6.3 Deadlock Resolution: Unresolved deadlocks after 15 days of good faith negotiation shall be referred to an agreed-upon neutral advisor.

7. NON-COMPETE AND NON-SOLICITATION
7.1 During the term of this Agreement and for 12 months post-departure, each Co-Founder shall not:
    (a) Start or join any competing AI startup in India
    (b) Solicit Company employees, clients, or investors
7.2 This clause shall not apply if a Co-Founder is terminated without cause.

8. CONFIDENTIALITY
Each Co-Founder shall maintain strict confidentiality of Company information during and for 3 years after departure.

9. DEPARTURE OF A CO-FOUNDER
9.1 A Co-Founder may resign with 3 months' written notice.
9.2 Upon departure, unvested shares are forfeited. Vested shares are retained subject to right of first refusal by the Company and remaining Co-Founder.
9.3 Right of First Refusal: Before transferring any shares to a third party, the departing Co-Founder must first offer them to the Company at fair market value.

10. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
Disputes shall be resolved by mediation first, followed by arbitration in Bengaluru under the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996.

11. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement is governed by Indian law, including the Companies Act, 2013.

12. ENTIRE AGREEMENT
This Agreement, along with the Shareholders' Agreement to be executed upon incorporation, constitutes the full understanding between the Co-Founders.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the Co-Founders have signed this Agreement on the date first written above.
