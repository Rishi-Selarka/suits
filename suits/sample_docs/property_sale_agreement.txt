AGREEMENT FOR SALE OF IMMOVABLE PROPERTY

This Agreement for Sale ("Agreement") is made on this 20th day of February, 2025, at Mumbai, Maharashtra, between:

VENDOR: Mr. Suresh Narayanan, son of Mr. P. Narayanan, aged 58 years, residing at Flat No. 12B, Sunshine Towers, Bandra West, Mumbai 400050, PAN: ABCPN1234D ("Vendor")

AND

PURCHASER: Mr. Aditya Mehta and Mrs. Kavya Mehta, son/daughter of Mr. R. Mehta, residing at 7, Rose Villa, Juhu, Mumbai 400049, PAN: XYZPM5678F / XYZPK9012G ("Purchaser")

WHEREAS the Vendor is the sole and absolute owner of the property described hereunder and desires to sell the same, and the Purchaser desires to purchase the same on the terms and conditions set out herein.

PROPERTY DESCRIPTION:
Residential flat bearing No. 403, on the 4th Floor, in the building known as "Emerald Heights", situated at Plot No. 47, S.V. Road, Andheri West, Mumbai 400058, admeasuring 1,200 square feet (carpet area 980 square feet), along with one covered car parking space No. 18 in the basement ("Property").

1. CONSIDERATION
1.1 The total sale consideration for the Property is INR 1,85,00,000 (Rupees One Crore Eighty-Five Lakhs only) ("Sale Consideration").
1.2 Payment Schedule:
   - Token Amount (paid on execution): INR 5,00,000
   - On registration of Agreement for Sale: INR 30,00,000
   - Balance on or before possession: INR 1,50,00,000

2. POSSESSION
2.1 The Vendor shall hand over vacant possession of the Property to the Purchaser within 90 days from the date of this Agreement.
2.2 Possession shall be subject to receipt of full Sale Consideration.
2.3 Delay in possession beyond 90 days shall attract compensation of INR 10,000 per day.

3. TITLE AND ENCUMBRANCES
3.1 The Vendor represents and warrants that the Property is free from all encumbrances, charges, mortgages, and litigation.
3.2 The Vendor has clear, marketable, and absolute title to the Property.
3.3 If title is found defective, Vendor shall refund all amounts received with 12% interest per annum.

4. OBLIGATIONS OF VENDOR
4.1 Execute and register the Sale Deed in favor of Purchaser upon receipt of full consideration.
4.2 Provide all original title documents, NOC from society, and occupation certificate.
4.3 Clear all outstanding dues (maintenance, property tax, utility bills) up to date of possession.
4.4 Obtain NOC from housing society for the transfer.

5. OBLIGATIONS OF PURCHASER
5.1 Pay the Sale Consideration as per the payment schedule.
5.2 Pay stamp duty and registration charges as applicable under Maharashtra Stamp Act.
5.3 Pay society transfer charges as per society bye-laws.
5.4 Complete the transaction within the stipulated timeframe.

6. STAMP DUTY AND REGISTRATION
6.1 Stamp duty, registration fees, and all other charges for registration of the Sale Deed shall be borne by the Purchaser.
6.2 Both parties shall cooperate for registration of the Sale Deed before the Sub-Registrar of Assurances.

7. DEFAULT
7.1 If Purchaser defaults in payment: Vendor may forfeit 10% of Sale Consideration (INR 18,50,000) as liquidated damages and cancel this Agreement.
7.2 If Vendor defaults: Purchaser shall be entitled to specific performance of this Agreement and/or refund of all amounts paid with 15% interest per annum.

8. BROKERAGE
8.1 The brokerage/commission shall be paid by the Vendor to the broker, if any.
8.2 The Purchaser has no obligation to pay any brokerage.

9. GOVERNING LAW
9.1 This Agreement shall be governed by the Transfer of Property Act, 1882, Registration Act, 1908, and laws of Maharashtra.
9.2 Disputes shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of courts in Mumbai.

10. MISCELLANEOUS
10.1 Time is the essence of this Agreement.
10.2 This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the parties.
10.3 Any amendment must be in writing and signed by both parties.
