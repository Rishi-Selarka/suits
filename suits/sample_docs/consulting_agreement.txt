MANAGEMENT CONSULTING AGREEMENT

This Consulting Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on 15th February 2026 between:

CLIENT: Meridian Industries Ltd., Plot 12, Andheri East, Mumbai – 400093 ("Client")
CONSULTANT: StratEdge Advisory LLP, 9th Floor, UB City, Bengaluru – 560001 ("Consultant")

1. ENGAGEMENT SCOPE
The Consultant is engaged to provide management consulting services as follows:
- Organizational restructuring strategy for the Client's manufacturing division
- Process efficiency audit across 3 plant locations
- Implementation roadmap and change management support
- Monthly progress reports and quarterly board presentations

Detailed scope is set out in the Statement of Work ("SOW") attached as Annexure A.

2. TERM
This Agreement shall be effective from 1st March 2026 to 28th February 2027 (12 months), unless terminated earlier.

3. FEES
3.1 The Client shall pay a monthly retainer of INR 8,00,000.
3.2 Travel, accommodation, and out-of-pocket expenses (with receipts) shall be reimbursed at cost, subject to a cap of INR 1,50,000 per month.
3.3 Invoices shall be raised on the 1st of each month and are due within 15 days.
3.4 Late payments attract interest at 18% per annum.
3.5 Any additional work beyond the SOW shall be billed at INR 25,000 per consultant day.

4. DELIVERABLES AND TIMELINES
Deliverables and milestones are as specified in Annexure A. Delays caused by the Client's failure to provide timely access, data, or approvals shall not constitute a breach by the Consultant.

5. ACCEPTANCE OF DELIVERABLES
5.1 The Client shall review and accept or reject deliverables within 10 business days of receipt.
5.2 Silence beyond 10 business days constitutes acceptance.
5.3 Rejection must be in writing with specific, actionable feedback.
5.4 The Consultant is entitled to two revision rounds per deliverable.

6. CONFIDENTIALITY
6.1 Both parties agree to keep confidential all proprietary information exchanged under this Agreement.
6.2 The Consultant may reference the engagement in its portfolio, using only anonymized information, unless the Client consents to being named as a reference.
6.3 This obligation survives termination for 3 years.

7. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
7.1 Deliverables created specifically for the Client become Client's property upon full payment.
7.2 The Consultant retains ownership of all pre-existing methodologies, frameworks, tools, and templates.
7.3 The Consultant grants the Client a non-exclusive, non-transferable license to use Consultant's methodologies embedded in deliverables for internal purposes only.

8. NON-SOLICITATION
During the term and for 18 months post-termination, the Client shall not directly solicit or hire any employee or associate of the Consultant. Violation shall attract liquidated damages of INR 50,00,000 per breach.

9. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
9.1 The Consultant's total liability shall not exceed 3 months' retainer fees.
9.2 The Consultant shall not be liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential losses.
9.3 The Consultant's recommendations are advisory only; the Client bears full responsibility for implementation decisions.

10. INDEMNIFICATION
The Client shall indemnify the Consultant against claims arising from the Client's misuse of deliverables or false information provided to the Consultant.

11. TERMINATION
11.1 Either party may terminate with 60 days' written notice.
11.2 The Client may terminate for material breach if the breach is not cured within 15 days of notice.
11.3 The Consultant may terminate immediately for non-payment beyond 30 days.
11.4 Upon termination, all fees for work completed are due within 30 days.

12. GOVERNING LAW AND DISPUTE RESOLUTION
This Agreement is governed by Indian law. Disputes shall first be mediated, then resolved by arbitration in Mumbai under the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996.
