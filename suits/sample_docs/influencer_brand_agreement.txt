INFLUENCER BRAND COLLABORATION AGREEMENT

This Agreement is made on 20th March 2026 between:

BRAND: GlowUp Cosmetics Pvt. Ltd., 12th Floor, Express Trade Tower, Noida – 201301 ("Brand")
INFLUENCER: Mr. Karan Bhatia (Instagram: @karanbhatia_official, 1.2M followers), residing at 7, Prithviraj Road, New Delhi – 110011 ("Influencer")

1. SCOPE OF COLLABORATION
The Influencer agrees to create and publish the following content for the Brand:
- 3 Instagram feed posts featuring Brand products
- 5 Instagram Stories (with swipe-up link)
- 1 YouTube video (minimum 8 minutes) with dedicated Brand segment
- 2 Instagram Reels (minimum 30 seconds each)

All content must feature the Brand's products as listed in Annexure A.

2. TERM
This Agreement shall be effective from 20th March 2026 to 30th June 2026.

3. COMPENSATION
3.1 Total fees: INR 4,50,000 (Rupees Four Lakh Fifty Thousand), exclusive of GST.
3.2 Payment schedule:
    - 50% on signing (INR 2,25,000)
    - 50% within 7 days of final content delivery
3.3 All taxes, including TDS at applicable rates (10%), shall be deducted by the Brand.

4. CONTENT APPROVAL
4.1 The Influencer shall submit all content drafts to the Brand at least 5 business days before the scheduled posting date.
4.2 The Brand has the right to request up to 2 rounds of revisions per content piece.
4.3 The Influencer shall not publish any content without explicit written approval from the Brand.
4.4 All content must comply with the ASCI (Advertising Standards Council of India) guidelines and include proper paid partnership disclosures.

5. EXCLUSIVITY
5.1 During the term of this Agreement, the Influencer shall not collaborate with or promote any direct competitors of the Brand (as listed in Annexure B) in the cosmetics and skincare category.
5.2 This exclusivity applies for 60 days after the term expires.

6. CONTENT OWNERSHIP AND LICENSE
6.1 The Influencer retains ownership of the content created.
6.2 The Influencer grants the Brand a non-exclusive, royalty-free, worldwide license to repurpose, republish, and use the content for digital marketing for 12 months from the posting date.
6.3 The Brand shall credit the Influencer when reusing content on the Brand's own channels.

7. PERFORMANCE REQUIREMENTS
7.1 The Influencer shall maintain minimum engagement metrics as agreed in Annexure C.
7.2 If a post fails to achieve 50% of the agreed minimum reach within 7 days, the Influencer shall create a replacement post at no additional cost.

8. REPRESENTATIONS
The Influencer represents that:
(a) Follower count and engagement metrics provided are genuine
(b) The Influencer has not purchased followers or engagement
(c) The Influencer has full authority to enter this Agreement
(d) No existing agreement prevents the Influencer from fulfilling obligations herein

9. MORALITY CLAUSE
The Brand may terminate this Agreement immediately if the Influencer is involved in any controversy, scandal, or public statement that the Brand deems harmful to its reputation, at the Brand's sole discretion. No compensation shall be due upon such termination.

10. TERMINATION
10.1 Either party may terminate with 14 days' written notice.
10.2 Upon termination, the Influencer shall remove any content posted under this Agreement if requested by the Brand.
10.3 Fees for work completed prior to termination shall be pro-rated.

11. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement is governed by Indian law. Disputes shall be subject to the jurisdiction of courts in Delhi.
